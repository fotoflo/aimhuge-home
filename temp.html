Worked in one-of-a-kind Fates & Graces cake dyed gradients, this shawl’s central motif of concentric bands of five colors radiates outwards and repeats, giving each color a chance to shine twice. Garter stitch ridges separate each color, adding beautiful surface texture and creating a pleasing structure of stripes and angles.

Skill Level: Intermediate

Skills Needed: Knit, purl, Judy’s Magic Cast on, increases (m1R, m1L, yo).

Finished Size: 56.5 inches / 144 cm wide, 18.5 inches / 47 cm long.

Yarn: MC - Infinite Twist Helix, Fates and Graces gradient cakes, 600 yards / 200 g. CC – Infinite Twist Helix, 500 yards / 167 g.

Shown in MC Blue Margarita with CC Dovecote.

Needles: Three U.S. size 5 / 3.75 mm circular, 32 inch / 80 cm long, or any size yielding correct gauge.

Optional: Three size 5 / 3.75 mm double-pointed needles.

Gauge (before blocking): 19 stitches / 40 rows over 4 inches / 10 cm in stockinette stitch after blocking.

Notions: 10 Stitch markers; at least one locking type/removable, yarn needle. Optional: point protectors if using DPNs.
