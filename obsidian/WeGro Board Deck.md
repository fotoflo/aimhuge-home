# WeGro Board Deck

Interactive HTML board presentation for [[WeGro]] investors and board members.

## Files

- `clients/wegro/index.html` — primary deck
- `clients/wegro/board-deck.html` — board presentation variant
- `clients/wegro/board-deck-v2.html` — v2 iteration

## Deck Design System

Uses a parallel design system from the main website:

- **Font**: Inter (not Fira Sans)
- **Primary green**: `#10b981` (Tailwind emerald-500)
- **Color coding**: Amber, Blue, Red, Purple for business divisions
- **CSS custom properties**: `--g500`, `--g700`, `--g800`, `--slate-*`
- **Features**: Intersection Observer animations, score bars, stat counters

## Key Content

- Company overview and vision
- Business line breakdown (Inputs, Financing, Trading, Data)
- Financial highlights ($11M+ financed)
- Impact metrics and SDG alignment
- Investment product showcase
- Partner bank logos
- Team and contact information
