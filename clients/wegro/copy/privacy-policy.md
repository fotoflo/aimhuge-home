# Privacy Policy

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy governs how WeGro collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users on the https://www.wegro.global website ("Site"). This privacy policy applies to the Site and all products and services offered by WeGro.

## Personal Identification Information

Personal identification information from users is collected in several ways, including when users visit the platform, register, place an order, and fill out questionnaires. Users provide Name, email address, phone number, Date of Birth, NID details, Financial Information (Bank Name, Branch and Account Number), and Nominee Information. Users also have the opportunity to visit the platform(s) anonymously.

## Non-personal Identification Information

Non-personal identification information may be collected about users as they visit the platform(s). Non-personal identification information includes (but is not limited to) browser name, device, operating system, and other technical information.

## Cookies

To provide a better consumer experience our platform(s) and mobile application use 'cookies'. Our platform(s) places cookies on their hard drive for record-keeping purposes and sometimes to track generic aggregated demographic information.

## How We Use Collected Information

WeGro collects and uses personal information to: display content correctly, improve customer experience, respond to service requests, ensure efficient service, personalize user experience, improve the platform, and process payment records.

## How We Protect Your Information

Proper measures are taken to prevent unauthorized data access by ensuring proper collection, storage, processing practices, and security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of personal information.

## Share of Personal Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent users' personal identification information to others. Certain generic aggregated demographic information regarding visitors and users can be shared with our business partners, trusted affiliates, and advertisers.

## Electronic Newsletters

Users may receive emails including company news, updates, and related product information if they've shared their email address on the platform.

## Third-Party Website

WeGro does not control the content or links on these external sites and is not accountable for the practices employed by websites linked to or from our platform.

## Disclosure Required by Law

We may disclose Personally Identifiable Information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to conform to the requirements of the law or comply with the legal process served on us.

## Data Retention

We will retain the information until the user requests to remove their data from the platform(s) or as needed to provide you with our services or comply with our legal and statutory obligations.

## Changes to Policy

WeGro has the discretion to update its privacy policy at any time. In case of a major update, we will post a notification on the main page of any of our platforms.

## Acceptance of Terms

By using this platform(s), your acceptance of this policy is accepted. Your continued use of the platform(s) following the posting of changes to this policy will be deemed your acceptance of those changes.

## Location Information

WeGro may collect and process location data from field agents using the OGRO Agent App to verify visits, track field activities, and ensure proper support for farmers.

**Purpose:** Location data verifies and records visit locations for field monitoring, improves service accuracy, and ensures transparency and accountability.

**Data Collected:** The app may collect precise GPS coordinates when active and approximate location in the background for field movement monitoring.

**Retention:** Location data is stored securely in our system and used strictly for internal operational purposes. Data is retained only as long as required to fulfill monitoring or compliance needs and then permanently deleted or anonymized.

**Consent:** By using the OGRO Agent App, users acknowledge consent to location data usage. Disabling location permissions in device settings is possible, though some functionalities may not work properly.

**Data Sharing:** WeGro does not share location data to third parties except when required by law or to authorized service providers under confidentiality obligations.

**Security:** WeGro applies strict data protection protocols and encryption measures to safeguard your location and other personal data from unauthorized access or misuse.
