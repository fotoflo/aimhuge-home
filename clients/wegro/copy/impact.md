## WeGro's Impact

Apart from ensuring access to finance, input resources, and markets; we are striving everyday toward our vision to make agriculture simplified in the hands of every farmer.

### 1.3 Times Income Uplift

Through innovative solutions and sustainable practices, we have empowered smallholder farmers to increase their earnings substantially, transforming livelihoods and lives.

### 21% Female Farmers

We are empowering women by fostering inclusively in farming communities- 21% of the farmers who have benefited from our initiatives are women.

### 60% Faster Loans

For smallholder farmers struggling with delayed loans, we have revolutionized access to credit, delivering loans faster than ever to support timely investment and growth.

### 25% Less Middleman Dependency

Instead of having to go through an average of 9 middlemen and 4-5 layers of the supply chain in the existing market, we have ensured farmers retain more of their hard-earned income by creating easier market access.

## SDG Impact

The activities we undertake everyday are helping to achieve the following Sustainable Development Goals as defined by the United Nations.

### SDG 1 - No Poverty
To alleviate smallholder farmers within our network from poverty by improving their lives and livelihoods.

### SDG 2 - Zero Hunger
To create improvements in the food supply value chain by introducing our technologies and infrastructure to the farmers

### SDG 3 - Good Health and Well-Being
To educate farmers by providing them with specialized training and mentorship for increasing financial literacy and improving technology adaptability.

### SDG 5 - Gender Equality
To foster inclusively in the farming community by empowering female farmers through financial as well as social aid and giving them a strong position in their community.

### SDG 8 - Decent Work and Economic Growth
To enhance overall economic growth of farmers by creating employment opportunities within the agriculture industry.

### SDG 10 - Reduced Inequalities
To reduce inequalities by ensuring that marginalized communities, particularly those in rural areas, have access to equal economic opportunities

## Our Commitment to Gender Equality

We are dedicated to empowering women in rural agriculture. We believe that supporting female farmers is essential for sustainable development. By providing equal opportunities and resources, we aim to uplift women's voices and leadership in the agricultural sector, fostering a more equitable future for all.

## Our Commitment

At WeGro, we are dedicated to transforming agriculture by connecting investors with smallholder farmers. Together, we cultivate sustainable growth, drive food security, and uplift communities, ensuring every investment contributes to a better future for all.

### Empowering Smallholder Farmers

We are committed to providing smallholder farmers with easy and steady access to finance and markets, enabling them to thrive economically.

### Uplifting Farmers' Lives

Addressing the challenges of the smallholder farmers, we want to support their growth by improving their lives. Through access to financial resources, training, and tools, we aim to enhance their livelihoods, reduce hardships, and ensure that the farmers' success remains at the heart of everything we do.

### Supporting Food Security

We are committed to enhancing productivity by providing access to innovative agricultural resources to improve the overall food supply chain, ultimately ensuring food security for the farmers.

### Fostering Community Engagement

Through workshops and volunteer opportunities, and valuing community engagement, we are committed to fostering collaboration.
