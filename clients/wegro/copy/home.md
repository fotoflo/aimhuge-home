# WeGro - Agriculture Investment Platform

## Main Tagline
"Agriculture Simplified."

## Company Overview

WeGro Technologies Limited is an agri-fintech company transforming rural farming in Bangladesh through digital finance, quality inputs, and market access. Founded in 2021, the company replaces high-interest informal loans with a structured, transparent financing model.

## Key Statistics & Impact

- **$11M+** financed to date
- **20,000+** farmers impacted across 43 upazilas
- **Global Hackcelerator Winner** (SFF 2024)

3-month cultivation project offering 4.20% – 5.50% annual returns with BDT 10,000 minimum investment. Emphasizes sustainable farming practices and quality produce with consistent market linkage.
