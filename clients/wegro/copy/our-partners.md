### Partnership Models

**Input Providers**
Support farmers with high-quality seeds, fertilizers, micro-nutrients, and essential resources to boost agricultural productivity.

**Market Partners**
Expand opportunities by connecting farmers to reliable markets for crops, cattle, fisheries, and other agricultural products.

**Banking Institutions**
Partner with WeGro to provide Sharia-compliant financial products, credit facilities, and investment opportunities tailored for smallholder farmers.

**Technology Providers**
Integrate cutting-edge tools and digital solutions to optimize agricultural operations and enhance transparency for farmers and investors.
